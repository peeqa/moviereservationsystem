☆ Memory Cards ☆


How to Play Memory Card Game and Win Free Popcorn:


1. Objective: The objective of the game is to match pairs of cards until all cards on the board are revealed.

2. Getting Started: To begin, click or tap on any card to reveal its image. Remember the position of the images as you'll need to find matching pairs.

3. Matching Pairs: To find matching pairs, click or tap on another card. If the images on the two cards match, they will remain face up.
If not, both cards will be flipped face down again, and you'll need to remember their positions for future turns.

4. Strategy: Remember the positions of the images on the cards to improve your chances of finding matching pairs. Concentration and focus are key!

5. Winning Points: For every pair of cards you match correctly, you earn one point.

6. Claiming Your Prize: Once you accumulate 15 points, you win a free regular popcorn! Take a screenshot of the game page showing your score
and show it to the staff to claim your prize.

7. Enjoy Your Popcorn: After claiming your prize, sit back, relax, and enjoy your delicious free regular popcorn as a reward for your memory skills!


Remember to have fun while playing the game and good luck matching those pairs!
